Key Cases (Case Stubs)

The DFU framework is built on the comparative analysis of these specific case records:

1. The Ultraman Case (China)

Guangzhou Internet Court, (2024) Yue 0192 Min Chu No. 113. (Holding that the AI generation of Ultraman images infringed reproduction/adaptation rights, establishing the "four-context analysis").

2. Kadrey v. Meta (U.S.)

Kadrey v. Meta Platforms, Inc., 85 F.4th (9th Cir. 2024) (or N.D. Cal. docket). (Discussing the "market dilution" theory under the fourth fair use factor).

3. Bartz v. Anthropic (U.S.)

Bartz v. Anthropic PBC, Case No. 3:24-cv-0476 (N.D. Cal. 2024). (Raising issues of training data and intermediate copying).

Guidance on Pincites

Since the arXiv version is a pre-print without traditional journal pagination, please cite by section heading (e.g., "see Section III.A") or paragraph number if counting from the start of the Introduction.
